Tramontana

Interactive kit for simple and rich multidevice experiences.

What is Tramontana?

Tramonatana is a tool for artists and designers to create interactive spaces and prototypes.

From one sketch you will be able to easily pilot a multitude of devices running iOS, tvOS or macOS.

Choose a sketching environment between:
· Processsing;
· OpenFrameworks;
· Javascript (web);

From the sketching environment you will be able to control one or more devices running iOS, tvOS or macOS as well as receiving input data from touchscreens, accelerometers, proximity sensors etc..



You can download for free the node apps for:
· iOS;
· macOS;
· search Tramontana on your appleTv to find tramontana for tvOS.

From your sketching environment you can control:
• flash light (iOS only);
• vibration (iOS only);
• changes to screen brightness and color;
• playback of video, audio or images;

Alternatively you can use the device (iOS only) as a sensor reading the device:
• orientation;
• magnetometer;
• accelerometer;
• distance sensor;
• audio jack;
• changes in power source;
• touch events;

To get started download the app and select your preferred platform, Javascript, openFrameworks or Processing and follow the instructions on the plugin or libraries page.
